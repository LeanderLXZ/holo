# Requirements <!-- holo:heading -->

<!-- holo:section start -->
Long-form, authoritative requirements document. This is the source of
truth; `ai_context/requirements.md` is its compressed index.

Two-layer separation:

- **This file** (`docs/requirements.md`) — full requirement statements,
  acceptance criteria, edge cases, rationale. May be long. May be
  written in the project's working language (not necessarily English).
- **`ai_context/requirements.md`** — compressed index, 2–5 lines per
  section, points back here with `→ docs/requirements.md §N`.

The pair is one row of `ai_context/conventions.md` §Cross-File
Alignment — change either one and the other must move in lockstep.
